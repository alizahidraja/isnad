Significance
The three standing modes in this example were produced by maintaining the tension in the string and adjusting the
driving frequency. Keeping the tension in the string constant results in a constant velocity. The same modes could
have been produced by keeping the frequency constant and adjusting the speed of the wave in the string (by
changing the hanging mass.)
